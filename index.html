I am Buwei He, a fourth-year Ph.D. candidate in Biostatistics at University of South Carolina, advised by Prof. Jiajia Zhang. I received my M.S. from Rutgers University and B.S. from Beijing Forestry University. My research interests lie at the intersection of statistical methodology and real-world data applications. Methodologically, I am interested in nonparametric statistical inference, high-dimensional data analysis, survival models, and machine learning. From the applied perspective, I focus on developing and implementing statistical approaches for analyzing complex electronic health record (EHR) data to advance evidence-based medical research and healthcare decision-making.
