关于我

About Me

我是何不为，毕业于美国南卡罗莱纳大学（University of South Carolina）生物统计学专业，获博士学位，导师为 Jiajia Zhang 教授。此前，我分别于罗格斯大学获得硕士学位， 于北京林业大学获得学士学位。我的研究兴趣主要集中在统计方法学与真实世界数据分析的交叉领域。在方法学方面，我关注非参数统计推论、高维数据分析、生存分析以及机器学习等方向； 在应用方面，我致力于开发和应用先进的统计方法，对复杂电子健康记录（EHR）数据进行分析，以推动循证医学研究和医疗健康决策的发展。

I am Buwei He, a recent Ph.D. graduate in Biostatistics from the University of South Carolina, where I was advised by Prof. Jiajia Zhang. I received my M.S. from Rutgers University and my B.S. from Beijing Forestry University. My research interests lie at the intersection of statistical methodology and real-world data applications. On the methodological side, I am interested in nonparametric statistical inference, high-dimensional data analysis, survival analysis, and machine learning. From an applied perspective, I focus on developing and implementing statistical methods for analyzing complex electronic health record (EHR) data to advance evidence-based medical research and healthcare decision-making.
